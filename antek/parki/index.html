In Pruszków there are 5 different parks. Their names are:

  • Żwirowisko Park
  • Anielin Park
  • Kościuszko Park
  • Mazovian Park
  • Potulickich Park


Project about the Mazovian park.

In the Mazovian park there are a lot of lakes and ducks in it. There are alleys and playgrounds. People there can fish and walk. Every weekend a lot of people come. Children can play on the playgrounds, and adults can walk along the alleys. It's very cool there!


History:

Area of the current Mazovian Park was formerly clay pit ponds. Hoser's family bought this terrain. Founded there forest schools and plantation of plants. After 1945, the government took over part of the Hoser's property and some of the land was sold. This area was purchased by the city of Pruszków.




What you can do in the Mazovian Park:


In the Mazovian Park we can swim in the summer, walk in the alleys and play in the playground. It's a nice place to spend time with your family and take a sip of fresh air.


Project about the Potulickich park.

Potulickich Park is the oldest park in Pruszków. It was created in XIX century. Until 1945 it belonged to Potulicki family. It is a reason for it's name.


Park has 23.25ha in size. It has a lot of lakes, ponds, bridges and a big fountain. All those add to 8.5ha.



In Potulickich Park you can walk, rent a kayak, go to the restaurant or look at natural monuments (very old trees). You can spend a nice time with your family. Every Saturday at 9am you can practice jogging joining a regular 5km „Parkrun” event.
